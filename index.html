Australia's Health Barometer & the effect of chronic diseases: 2010-2020

Introduction

The given dataset shows an overview of health status of Australian(2010-2020) It also shows the proportion of chronic longterm diseases by state and as a whole in Australia. Laslty, it displays the most major chronic diseas:Ischaemic heart disease amongst Australians, with special emphasis on NSW and Victoria.

First section: Proportion of physically fit Australians: 2019

The choropleth map shows the proportion of physically fit residents by each state. The data is collected by ABS survey for 2019-2020.

Second section: Least to most healthy and No. of chronic conditions: Statewise(2017-2018)

The first column shows the stacked bar chart. Stacked bar chart compares using the part to whole relationship for each state and across each state giving a better idea of which state has least and most healthy residents according to the dataset. Heat map shows proportion of people by number of chronic, conditions diagnosed per state in 2017-2018.

Third section: Impact of long term conditions in Australia and Victoria

Bar chart shows Proportion of long term conditions in persons 15 and above (%)- 2017-2018 residents over whole of Australia. The pie shows part to whole relationship of long term chronic conditions with death rate in Victoria 2020.
