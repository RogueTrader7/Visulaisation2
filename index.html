Australia's Health Barometer: 2010-2020

Introduction

The given dataset shows an overview of the health barometer of Australians (2010-2020)

It shows the proportion of chronic longterm diseases by state and as a whole in Australia. Laslty, it displays the most major chronic diseases termed as "CHAPTER IX Diseases of the circulatory system (I00-I99)", particularly Ischaemic heart disease amongst Australians, with special emphasis on Victoria.

Section 1 : How many Australians are physically fit?

The choropleth map shows the proportion of physically fit residents by each state. The data is collected by ABS survey for 2019-2020.

Section 2 : So what's the health status by state?

Least to most healthy and No. of chronic conditions: Statewise(2017-2018)

While the stacked bar chart displays the proportion of excellent fitness to poor health state wise, the heat map provides further emphasis on no. of chronic conditions affecting each resident per state.Heat map shows proportion of people by number of chronic, conditions diagnosed per state in 2017-2018.

Section 3 : Impact of chronic conditions on Australia and Victoria

Bar chart shows Proportion of long term conditions in persons 15 and above (%)- 2017-2018 residents in Australia. The pie shows part to whole relationship of long term chronic conditions contributing to death rate in Victoria 2020.

Section 4 : Diseases of the circulatory system(Chapter IX of ICD)

First Visualisation shows deaths in Victoria and Australia by diseases of the circulatory system. Second visualisation shows deaths by Ischaemic heart disease in Australia by Males and Females
